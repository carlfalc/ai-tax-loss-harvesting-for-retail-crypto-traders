Frequently Asked Questions

How does CryptoHarvest avoid wash sale violations?
Our AI tracks all your transactions across connected accounts and ensures any rebuy orders are placed after the required 31-day period, or uses substantially identical assets to maintain market exposure while staying compliant.
Is my portfolio data secure?
Yes, we use bank-level encryption and only require read-only API access to your accounts. We never have access to your funds or private keys, and all data is stored securely with SOC 2 compliance.
Which exchanges and wallets are supported?
We support 20+ major exchanges including Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, FTX, and KuCoin, plus popular wallets like MetaMask, Ledger, and Trezor. We're constantly adding new integrations.
How much can I expect to save in taxes?
Tax savings vary based on your trading activity and tax bracket, but our users typically save 15-30% on their crypto tax liability. The average user with a $100K portfolio saves $8,000-12,000 annually.
Can I use this for DeFi positions?
Yes, our Professional and Enterprise plans include DeFi protocol support for popular platforms like Uniswap, Compound, and Aave. We track LP positions, yield farming, and staking rewards.
